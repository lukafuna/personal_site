My Travels

Barcelona

Barcelona was a vibrant feast for the senses, where every street seemed to tell a story. First I visited the iconic Sagrada Família, mesmerized by Gaudí’s architectural genius. Then a walk through Park Güell, I loved the colorful mosaics and designs, feeling like I had stepped into a fairytale. La Rambla had amazing energy, filled with street performers, markets, and delicious tapas. The beaches offered an escape, where the Mediterranean waves splashed on the golden sands. Barcelona’s blend of modernist art, historic charm, and lively atmosphere left an indelible mark on my heart.

Rome

Rome was like stepping back in time, where ancient history and modern life intertwine seamlessly. The Colosseum stood majestic, a testament to the grandeur of the Roman Empire, while the Roman Forum whispered tales of politics and philosophy. The Vatican City was awe-inspiring, with St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel showcasing unparalleled artistry. Wandering through the narrow cobblestone streets, I discovered quaint piazzas, each with its own unique character and charm. The cuisine was a highlight, with every meal – from pasta to gelato – being a delightful culinary adventure. Rome’s eternal beauty and historical richness made every moment unforgettable.

Paris

Paris was a dream come true, a city where romance and elegance are the fabric of everyday life. The Eiffel Tower’s lights in the night sky were a sight to behold, beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré. The Louvre housed countless masterpieces all of artistic evolution and human expression. Strolling along the Seine, I enjoyed looking at the beautiful bridges and historic landmarks, from Notre-Dame to the bookstalls of the Left Bank. The cafés, offered the perfect spot to enjoy a croissant and watch the world go by. I love that experience and I will cherish it forever.
